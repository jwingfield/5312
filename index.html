Bradford Based Classic Rock & Pop Covers

Songs from the 60s right up until now

Band History

Formed in 2003, 53/12 have some 10 years experience playing around the Bradford area, and were one of the first bands to play at the Saltaire Festival. We're taking things a little easier these days, but we're still available to play at your party, pub, beer festival or other event.
